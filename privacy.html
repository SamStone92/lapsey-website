Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Lapsey ("the App") is a timelapse camera application developed by Sam Stone. This policy explains what data the App collects and how it is used.

Data We Collect

Lapsey does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. All photos, videos, and project files created within the App are stored locally on your device and are never sent to any server.

Camera & Photo Library Access

The App requires access to your device camera to capture timelapse frames. Access to your Photo Library is requested only when you choose to save a rendered video. This access is used solely to perform the requested action and no data leaves your device.

Purchases

In-app purchases are handled entirely by Apple via the App Store. Lapsey does not have access to your payment information. Purchase records are managed by Apple in accordance with their privacy policy.

Analytics & Tracking

Lapsey does not use any third-party analytics, crash reporting, or advertising SDKs. No tracking occurs within the App.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. Because no data is collected at all, the App is safe for use by all ages.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be published at this URL. Continued use of the App following any changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to samstone920@gmail.com.

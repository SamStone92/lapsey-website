FAQ

Common questions.

What devices does Lapsey support?
Lapsey runs on any iPhone or iPad with iOS 17 or later. It's optimised for the full range of iPhone 15 and 16 cameras, including wide-angle and ultra-wide lenses.
Do I need to keep the app open while recording?
Yes — Lapsey captures frames using the live camera so the app needs to remain in the foreground. Plug in your device and disable auto-lock in Settings for long shoots.
What's the shortest interval I can use?
Lapsey can fire as frequently as every second, making it ideal for fast-moving subjects like traffic, weather, or crowds.
Can I use Lapsey for stop-motion animation?
Absolutely. The layer overlay feature lets you ghost the previous frame over the live view — the same technique animators use to keep motion smooth and consistent.
Is the £1.99 a one-time purchase or a subscription?
It's a one-time, non-renewing purchase. Pay once and unlock the full app permanently on all your devices.
How do I trigger shots with a game controller?
Connect an MFi-compatible, PlayStation, or Xbox controller to your iPhone via Bluetooth. Lapsey detects it automatically — press the trigger button to fire the shutter manually.
